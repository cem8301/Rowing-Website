Alumni



As rowers, we alumni have a good idea of the huge costs associated with the sports, and our goal is to ease the burden on the current and future athletes. It is this goal that has motivated us to create and pursue our core programs. All of these programs involve large sums of money, as well as people to organize, plan, and implement them. If you have time to donate and are interested in helping out, we encourage you to get involved.
