Colorado In the News

Four Year Rower Sam Tobey was awarded the University of Colorado Collegiate Sport Clubs Male Athlete of the Year. Sam has been one of the hardest working members of this program for the past four years, leading the team as president in the 2013-2014 season." "It is the behind the scenes stuff; the officers, the planning, the budget, and everything else that helps our teams be successful", said collegiate sport clubs director Patty McConnell.

CU Independent interviewed Zoe Roth and Claire Ochsner about their experiences on the team. Zoe Said, "The key to success is synchronization and rhythm. Once you’re in the boat you have to work as a complete unit. It doesn’t matter who is faster and who is slower, as long as you’re in sync and pulling hard.” For more information check out the article: CU Independent

CU-Boulder’s women’s novice 8+ rowing team took bronze medals in the finals of the ACRA (American Collegiate Rowing Association) national championships. Over 55 colleges and universities sent teams to the yearly championship, putting 1,350 athletes, both female and male, out on the water for two days of heats and final eliminations. Check out this article for more information: Boulder iJournal
