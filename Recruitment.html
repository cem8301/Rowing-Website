Recruitment





Are you interested in rowing for Colorado Crew? Recruiting for the 2014-15 season has begun! Come to Learn-to-Row Week during the first week of school, August 26th-30th, in the Rec Center's Rowing Studio. Varsity rowers will be there from 4:30 to 6:30 and teach in 1/2 hour shifts.Learn-to-Row Week helps introduce the sport of rowing to students of all backgrounds during daily sessions, given by the coaches and other rowers. No prior experience is necessary.

Our official information night is on Wednesday, August 27th at 8:30PM in Humanities 250. The Humanities building is located on the North side of the Norlin Quad.

Have more questions or comments? E-mail us at cucrewrecruiting@gmail.com.
