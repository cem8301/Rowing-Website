Rent-a-Rower



Do you have some heavy lifting, painting, moving, gardening, cleaning, or other work that needs tending to? Colorado Crew can help! In order to help us pay for our team's expenses, we "rent out" members of the team to people in the community through our Rent-a-Rower program. Rowers are disciplined workers, honest people, and able to tackle any job, whether it takes a couple of hours or multiple days. If you are interested in hiring one or more workers, contact the Vice President (Peter Graca) at crewjobs@colorado.edu.

If you are interested in hiring a worker contact us at crewjobs@colorado.edu

Rate - $65 dollars/4 hours.
